How To Apply

Applying for NASA Space Apps Borg El-Arab is a piece of cake,
and here are how can you do it with tips of your fingers.


Step 1: Browse the 23 challenges of NASA Space Apps, read them, pick the one you want to solve.
Step 2: Register at the main NASA Space Apps website and choose Borg El-Arab location from this link
Nasa spaceappschallenge - BORG AL ARAB
Step 3: start designing and working on the solution for your challenge, whether you choose to participate individually or in a team.
Step 4: register you idea and information at our form in the registration tab
Step 5: Wait for your acceptance to participate at NASA Space Apps Borg El-Arab
